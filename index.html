Summary

I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the University of Florence, focusing on distributed systems, blockchain, and smart contract engineering. I am also a Visiting Research Fellow at Brunel University London and a Research Fellow at the University of Camerino.

My academic interests include formal verification of smart contracts, modular design for decentralized applications, and process modeling using BPMN. I actively contribute to open-source Algorand development and engage in cross-institutional research collaborations.
