Summary

Mr. Nawaz Abdullah Malla is a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Florence, specializing in Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Technology. As a Visiting Research Fellow at Brunel University London, his research focuses on the engineering of trustworthy blockchain-aware applications, with particular emphasis on formal verification methods for smart contracts and decentralized systems. His work bridges theoretical foundations with practical implementations, developing rigorous techniques to enhance security and reliability in distributed applications. His work also investigates the intersection of large language models and smart contract generation, developing frameworks to enhance the reliability of AI-generated contract code through verification techniques.

With a strong academic foundation including an M.Tech in Computer Science (Gold Medalist) and research experience across international institutions, Mr. Malla actively contributes to the blockchain research community through conference presentations, scientific schools, and open-source development. His work aims to advance the state of blockchain engineering through practical yet theoretically sound solutions for real-world decentralized applications.
