About Me

I am Nawaz Abdullah Malla, a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science specializing in Blockchain Technology and Distributed Ledger Technology at the University of Florence, Italy. My research focuses on the foundational study of distributed systems and the application of smart contracts for developing decentralized applications.

With a strong academic background and industry experience, I bridge the gap between theoretical computer science and practical blockchain implementations. My work emphasizes the design of dynamic smart contracts and formal verification methods to enhance security and reliability in decentralized systems.
