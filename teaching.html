Teaching Activities

My teaching approach focuses on combining rigorous theoretical foundations with meaningful, hands-on experience to cultivate deep practical understanding. I aim to equip students with both conceptual understanding and the technical skills needed to build secure and scalable decentralized applications (DApps).

Project-Based Learning

Students engage in full-cycle DApp development, from smart contract design to blockchain deployment, using real-world case studies and industry-standard tools.

Security-First Approach

Courses embed formal verification principles early on, ensuring that students develop a security mindset while coding smart contracts and designing decentralized systems.
